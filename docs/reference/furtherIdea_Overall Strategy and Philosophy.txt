1.⁠ ⁠Overall Strategy & Philosophy: "The Asynchronous Mind"
To beat the human test, latency must stay under 500ms, interruptibility must be flawless, and the agent cannot ask "stupid questions" (asking for information the insurer already knows).

Our philosophy is "The Asynchronous Mind": We separate the conversational soul from the data extraction engine.

The Conversational Core: Powered by Google Deepmind (Gemini 3 Flash via AI Studio for ultra-low latency) + Gradium TTS via LiveKit. Its only job is to sound empathetic, handle interruptions, and keep the conversation flowing naturally.
The Background Brain: As the conversation happens, Gemini 1.5 Pro processes the transcript asynchronously to extract the 13 required Inca data points and pushes them to a real-time React dashboard built with Lovable.
The Infrastructure: We use Entire to document our AI-assisted development, Aikido to secure the cloud/repo, and Tavily MCP to give the agent real-time context (weather/maps).
