04 / 04 · beyond the conversation

THE RIGOR BEHIND THE VOICE

Security & privacy by default

12 PII patterns redacted before anything touches a log — policy #, VIN, plate, IBAN, credit card, SVNR, Krankenversichertennr, driver licence, phone, email, DOB. Insurance handles GDPR Article 9 health data; we treated it that way from commit one.

Provenance, not just commits

Every architectural decision is captured in a build journal alongside the diff — so the why of each change lives next to the what. Future maintainers don't have to guess.

Voice quality, three tiers

Laptop-mic prototype, direct-SDK filler-audio batcher, and a production telephony worker — all on the same voice (Emma family) with STT temperature pinned to suppress noise hallucination on highway / café audio.

Two extraction lanes

47 ms · free on-device named-entity extraction for speed, versus structured-output LLM at 300 ms · $0.0015 for accuracy. Same domain config drives both. The trade-off is a feature, not a hack.
